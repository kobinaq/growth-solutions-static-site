About Growth Solutions

We are a Ghanaian social enterprise committed to community-driven development. Our ethos is rooted in collaboration, transparency and an entrepreneurial spirit that transforms local ideas into sustainable impact.

Our Mission

At Growth Solutions, our mission is to co-create solutions with local communities that are environmentally sound, socially inclusive and economically empowering. We believe that sustainable development emerges when people have a voice and the tools to shape their own futures.

Guiding Principles

Local Partnership

We engage communities as equal partners, recognising that local knowledge drives long-term success.

Transparency & Openness

We communicate openly with partners, sharing information and results to foster trust and accountability.

Entrepreneurial Spirit

We approach challenges with creativity and a can-do attitude, turning ideas into opportunities.

Our Approach

  • We begin by listening carefully to communities to understand their priorities.
  • We facilitate participatory planning workshops to co-design solutions.
  • We draw on cross-sector partnerships to mobilise resources and expertise.
  • We monitor, evaluate and adapt to ensure that initiatives remain effective and scalable.
